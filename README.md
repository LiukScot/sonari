# Sonari

Ambient **background sound mixer** for Android — mix rain, waves, café, fireplace
and more to focus, relax or sleep. Inspired by the GNOME app
[Blanket](https://github.com/rafaelmardojai/blanket).

> 🚧 Early development. See [PLAN.md](PLAN.md) for the full design and roadmap.

## What makes it different
- **Customizable multi-mix** — several sounds at once, each with its own volume.
- **Quick Settings tiles** — start/stop a mix straight from the notification
  shade, and assign your saved presets to configurable tiles. (Most Blanket
  clones have no tile at all.)
- Seamless looping, audio focus, sleep timer, custom sounds.

## Tech
Kotlin · Jetpack Compose · Media3 (ExoPlayer) · minSdk 24.
